Bob Tianqi Wei

"Tyan-Chi", he/they (What is this?)

I am an HCI researcher, design engineer, and artist.

Currently I am an HCI Researcher at the Berkeley Institute of Design Lab, I work with Shm Almeda and Professor Björn Hartmann (EECS). I hold a Master's degree in Design and Human-Computer Interaction from the College of Engineering at UC Berkeley. I earned a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Design from Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Lintao Tang.

I have a strong foundation in design and manufacturing, EECS, mechanical engineering, and learning sciences, combined with my passion for music and contemporary art. My research explores innovative ways to enhance human interaction with the physical world, focusing on the interplay of technology, cognition, and emotion. Through a phenomenological lens, I aim to investigate how emerging technologies can transform music, art, creativity, and embodied interaction.
